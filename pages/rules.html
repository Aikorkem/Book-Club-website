Book Club Rules

  1. Read.

    A participant, who has not read the book more than three times, is excluded from the group.

  2. One mic rule.

    During the discussion everyone listens to the speaker attentively and should not interrupt him/her.

  3. Show respect.

    Do not use swear words, and treat each participant and his/her opinion with respect.

  4. Attendance.

    If the participant is absent from the meeting more than three times without a reason, the participant is excluded from the group.

  5. Don't be late.

    If you are late for a meeting without a reason, some functions of the book club, like voting for the best analysis and for the next book, are blocked.

  6. Group chat.

    It is forbidden to place advertising, spam and other material that is not related to the topic of the conversation in chat groups.
